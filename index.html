Immersive Education

immersive education transforms traditional learning by creating a virtual environment where students can interact with objects and receive AI-generated insights. This approach eliminates physical barriers, allowing disabled students to explore and engage with educational content in a sensory-rich, accessible setting. By leveraging AR/VR technology, our project fosters a dynamic and inclusive learning experience, enabling students to understand complex concepts through hands-on interaction and real-time adaptability.

Inclusive Virtual Learning Environment

We focus on creating an accessible virtual platform where disabled students can explore educational environments without physical barriers. This fosters inclusivity and ensures equal learning opportunities for all students.

Immersive AR/VR Integration

Using AR/VR technology, we are developing a sensory-rich experience that allows students to navigate and interact with 3D models. This immersive approach bridges the gap between theoretical learning and practical application.

AI-Driven Interactivity

By integrating AI, we enable real-time object recognition and description, allowing students to click on virtual objects and receive contextual information. This feature enhances engagement and comprehension.
